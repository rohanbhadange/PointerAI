Setup paths

Lowest friction

Local keys

Paste your OpenAI, AssemblyAI, and ElevenLabs keys into the app. PointerAI writes a local env file on your computer.

Secret isolation

Cloudflare Worker

Deploy your own Worker, store provider keys as Worker secrets, then paste the Worker URL into PointerAI.

User-owned access

Your keys. Your usage. Your setup.

PointerAI is designed around bring-your-own provider accounts. The app does not require a shared hosted API account, so early users can test without shifting usage costs onto the product owner.
